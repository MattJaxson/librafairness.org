## Short Note for Reviewers

Libra is a working demo of a community-governed AI fairness workflow. It demonstrates how a community-defined fairness standard can move from draft, to finalization, to signed receipt, to public registry, to vendor evidence review.

The District 3 / Emily material is a pre-session planning demo. The actual District 3 resident session has not happened yet, and the app should not be read as claiming a completed District 3 result.
